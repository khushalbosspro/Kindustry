Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

Kindustry is a gaming community platform operating at kindustry.in. We provide gaming tournaments, giveaways, community features, and a store. You can contact us at official@kindustry.in.

2. What Information We Collect

When you create an account we collect:

  • Your email address (via Google or email login)
  • Your chosen username
  • Your profile picture (if you upload one)
  • Your tournament participation and win history
  • Basic usage data (pages visited, time on site) for improving our services

We do not collect payment information, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display your profile and stats on the leaderboard
  • To contact you about tournaments, giveaways, and updates
  • To improve our website and services
  • To prevent abuse and enforce our community rules

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using enterprise-grade infrastructure with row-level security and industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest. We never sell your data to third parties. Authentication is powered by Google Sign-In and Discord — trusted industry-standard providers.

5. Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Authentication services for secure login
  • Database and storage services for your account data
  • Website hosting and security services
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve user experience
  • Contact form processing services

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To delete your account, go to your Profile → Settings → Delete Account. To request data deletion contact us at official@kindustry.in.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — for authentication sessions. We do not use advertising cookies or sell cookie data.

8. Contact

For any privacy questions contact us at official@kindustry.in or through our Discord server.

History

Update Catalog

Every update, improvement and milestone we've shipped — from day one to now.

✦
v0.5 Latest
Operation Community — Profiles, Identity & Launch Prep
June 2026
The biggest profile update yet. Players can now fully express themselves with bios, game preferences and social links. The OG Member badge rewards the first 1000 players to ever join Kindustry — a permanent mark of being here from the start. The site now nudges incomplete profiles, tournament cards got a visual upgrade, and Ko-fi support landed in the nav. Beta Season 0 is loading.
  • 🔥 OG Member badge — automatically awarded to the first 1,000 players to join Kindustry, permanent and gold-glowing
  • Profile completion nudge — players with missing bio, game preferences or social links are prompted to complete their profile
  • Tournament card hover glow — cards now lift and glow on hover for a more premium feel
  • ☕ Ko-fi support button added to the hero section — help keep Kindustry free and running
  • Profile bio, game preferences and social links system fully live
  • Public profile modal shows bio, games and socials for any player
  • Admin signup viewer — track every new member from the admin panel
  • Bell notifications system with real-time polling and z-index fix
  • Giveaway entry notifications delivered to players on entry
  • Candy theme added — vibrant pink and purple playful aesthetic
  • Glass theme added — frosted blur with neon accent styling
✦
v0.4 Milestone
Operation Ironclad — Accounts, Speed & Security
May 2026
The biggest infrastructure update Kindustry has ever seen. Player accounts got a massive upgrade — profile pictures now upload to our own cloud system, persist across every device you sign in from, and look sharp at any size. Security was completely overhauled with a multi-layer admin protection system so only authorized staff can access the control panel. We also introduced the Boost button — a one-click performance mode that makes the site silky smooth on any machine. Two brand new themes dropped, the glass theme got fixed, and a full UI pass cleaned up everything across the board.
  • Profile pictures now upload to our own cloud — stored permanently, loads on any device you sign in from without re-uploading
  • Smart image compression — uploaded photos are automatically optimized so the site stays fast even with thousands of player avatars
  • Profile picture upload restricted to registered Kindustry members only
  • ⚡ Boost Mode added to the navbar — one click disables heavy visual effects for smooth performance on any PC
  • Boost Mode remembers your choice — stays on even after refreshing the page
  • Admin panel security completely rebuilt — triple-layer verification replaces the old password system
  • Admin access now tied to verified accounts — typing someone's email does nothing without the actual account session
  • Added Teal theme — clean teal and cyan aesthetic
  • Added Venom theme — dark symbiote-inspired look
  • Fixed Glass theme visual glitches and blur rendering
  • Enhanced all UI elements — cards, buttons, transitions and spacing refined across every page
  • Fixed sign out and delete account flows
  • Updated Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
✦
v0.3 Milestone
Operation Polished — The UI Overhaul
May 2026
A full quality pass across the board. Fixed login bugs, enhanced every theme visually, improved animations and overhauled the overall user experience to feel more premium and smooth.
  • Fixed Discord OAuth login flow — users can now sign in with Discord reliably
  • Refined all 6 existing themes with better contrast, glows and card styles
  • Enhanced navbar with floating pill design and scroll animations
  • Improved achievement toasts and notification system
  • Fixed various UI inconsistencies across pages
  • Performance improvements and general polish across the board
✦
v0.2 Milestone
Identity Expansion — Themes & Authentication
April 2026
Kindustry got its identity. We launched our full authentication system letting players create accounts and pick usernames, and introduced four new visual themes giving everyone a way to make Kindustry feel personal.
  • Launched full login system — Google OAuth, Discord OAuth and Email sign in
  • Username picker with bad word filter, uniqueness check and 12 character limit
  • Added Cyber RGB theme — animated rainbow border cycling effect
  • Added White Pro theme — clean minimal light mode
  • Added Championship Gold theme — black and gold esports aesthetic
  • Fixed Google OAuth callback and session persistence bugs
  • Profile page with achievements, stats and settings tab
✦
v0.1 Origin
Genesis — Where It All Started
March 2026
The very first version of Kindustry. Built from scratch as a single HTML file, this was the foundation — the spark that started everything. Two themes, a homepage and a dream to build the ultimate gaming community.
  • Launched Kindustry.in — the gaming community website goes live
  • Built the core Dark Neon theme — deep blues, neon purples and glowing cards
  • Added Crimson theme — dark red esports energy aesthetic
  • Home page with hero section, features grid and Discord banner
  • Tournaments page with registration form and secure cloud database backend
  • Leaderboard page with live real-time database connection
  • Contact page with secure form submission integration
