Our Story

We Are Kindustry

"where everything forges into greatness"

Building the future of gaming communities.

Kindustry started as a Discord server with one idea — a space where gamers compete, connect, and grow together. No gatekeeping. No toxicity. Just pure gaming passion.

We run free monthly tournaments, give back through giveaways, and are building a full gaming ecosystem — digital stores, real-life merch, and competitive esports. Think Epic Games meets Steam, built for the world.

Our esports arm, Team GODK, is actively forming. We're assembling a squad to compete nationally. Tryouts coming very soon.
