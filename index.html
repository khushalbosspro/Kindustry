Our Story

We Are Kindustry

"where everything forges into greatness"

Building the future of gaming communities.

We run free monthly tournaments, give back through giveaways, and are building a full gaming ecosystem — digital stores, real-life merch, and competitive esports. Think Epic Games meets Steam, built for the world.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

Kindustry is a gaming community platform operating at kindustry.in. We provide gaming tournaments, giveaways, community features, and a store. You can contact us at officialkindustry@gmail.com.

2. What Information We Collect

When you create an account we collect:

  • Your email address (via Google or email login)
  • Your chosen username
  • Your profile picture (if you upload one)
  • Your tournament participation and win history
  • Basic usage data (pages visited, time on site) via Microsoft Clarity

We do not collect payment information, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display your profile and stats on the leaderboard
  • To contact you about tournaments, giveaways, and updates
  • To improve our website and services
  • To prevent abuse and enforce our community rules

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database with row-level security). We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest. We never sell your data to third parties.

5. Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Authentication services for secure login
  • Database and storage services for your account data
  • Website hosting and security services
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve user experience
  • Contact form processing services

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To delete your account, go to your Profile → Settings → Delete Account. To request data deletion contact us at officialkindustry@gmail.com.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — for authentication sessions. We do not use advertising cookies or sell cookie data.

8. Contact

For any privacy questions contact us at officialkindustry@gmail.com or through our Discord server.
